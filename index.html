WHO WE ARE

TTRIKON is all about creating the best experience for everyone who chooses to be a part of our journey, each and every time. ‘Going that extra mile’ for our clients (yes, that could be you!) is not just a catch phrase, but our core operating mantra.

We love what we do, and that’s why we are great at it! Proud to be travel junkies at heart, we are super-excited to share your journey and create magic together. We are diverse: You could be 5 years or 65 years old, first-timers or certified travel keedas, thrill lovers or just out to chill, travelling alone or with family or friends, with us you can find just the right type of plan to fit your imagination and needs. For us, fun is serious business, very serious. So rest assured, there is hardly a dull moment when you hop on aboard the TTRIKON wagon.

Your trip leaders or ‘ Trip Buddies’, as we prefer to call them, come packed with solid experience, meticulous planning and just the right amount of wackiness to help you create a ‘full paisa vasool’ experience! So, stay tuned as we will be soon sharing our action packed plans and, hope to see all of you joining us, kisi na kisi mod par!
